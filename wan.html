Wanaka Lake Photos

Photos from us on our glamping around Lake Wanaka

The landscape changed dramatically from one side of the lake to the other: one side was much drier, but a short 30 km drive around the lake provided yet another type of landscape. The night sky was amazing - so many stars! And we finally clearly saw the Southern Cross constellation.


The thumbnails do not show it, but click on the "dark black images" to see the southern constellations. Besides from being remote from the cities, you can see the stars very well.
