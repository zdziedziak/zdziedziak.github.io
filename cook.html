Mount Cook Photos

Photos from the Mount Cook area

Mount Cook is New Zealand's tallest mountain. We also took a boat tour in front of the Tasman Glacier (NZ's largest glacier). We rode around a small lake (the one with the grey-blue water, due to "rock flour" which is debris that the glacier pushes into the water), which did not exist before roughly 1990. If you look at the photos, there is a clear vegetation line - the glacier extended to that height around 45 years ago...

The Tasman Glacier feeds the Lake Pukaki (the more intense blue colored lake), which was the filming location of Lake-town in the movie, The Hobbit.
