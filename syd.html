Sydney Photos

Photos taken during our roughly 12 hour layover in Sydney

We walked around Sydney for the day and soaked up the Australian heat before we escaped to the more temperate NZ.


On our way back to California, we also had an 8 hour layover in Sydney, so we went to the Sydney Zoo

On the way back it was around 30 degrees Celsius (~85 Fahrenheit), so they animals were especially lazy. Don't blame them. It was hot (and humid)

I swear that all the animals were alive - they were just combating the heat. Must say, I prefer the NZ climate...
