Milford Track and Milford Sound Photos

Photos from us tramping through the Milford Track and the Milford Sound

It was an amazing, 33 mile hike! We saw great mountains, tons of waterfalls, rivers, glaciers, and cheeky birds!

One of the cheeky wekas (NZ chicken-rat bird) stole the meat out of somebody's sandwich. I caught it with the camera mid-theft (click on the larger picture to see it better)
