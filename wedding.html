Lodging Info:


INDIVIDUAL CABINS/HOUSES

You may wish to stay in individual cabins or houses. We recommend staying "on the summit" in Soda Springs or Norden, but you could also look down at Donner Lake or in Truckee. There are also individual cabins located on the Sugar Bowl property. For these, please contact Sugar Bowl resort directly or check out VRBO or AirBnB.

TRUCKEE DONNER LODGE

At the East end of Donner Lake (right next to the Donner Party museum) sits Truckee Donner Lodge. This venue is off of the summit, but we will be providing transportation to and from this lodge the day of the wedding. The discounted rates are $209 plus tax per night for a One King bed room and $239 plus tax per night for a Two Queen bed room. Please tell Celeste that you are calling for the Clark-Dziedziak Wedding. A hot breakfast is included with the room.

DONNER LAKE INN

This is a small five room bed and breakfast (two rooms are queen beds, two are queen + twin and one family room with a queen and a set of bunk beds for kids under 18). August is one of the busiest months of the year, so Paul encourages people to book early through their website. Queen rooms are $169 per night, Queen + twin $179 per night and the family room is $189-$199 per night. The rate includes parking, WiFi, hot tub, afternoon snack, and full hot cooked breakfast. Please tell Paul that you are calling for the Clark-Dziedziak Wedding.

LOCH LEVEN LODGE

Down the summit from the venue sits beautiful Donner Lake. With Truckee Taxi, Uber and Lyft readily available, Lock Leven is a great option for those looking for a quite hotel that has in-unit kitchens and with a beautiful of the lake. Prices vary from $130 to $320.
