Lodging Info:


We have reserved the lodge at Sugar Bowl for the entire weekend (August 9 – 12). There are only 27 rooms, so please email ashley.clark6@gmail.com if you need a room and indicate which nights you would like to stay. The rooms vary to accommodate single couples, families, or friends looking to share a room. We will be working with the hotel to assign the rooms, so please be sure to reserve your room as soon as possible. You may also wish to stay in individual cabins or houses. We recommend staying "on the summit" in Soda Springs or Norden, but you could also look down at Donner Lake or in Truckee. There are also individual cabins located on the Sugar Bowl property. For these, please contact Sugar Bowl resort directly or check out VRBO or AirBnB.

At the East end of Donner Lake (right next to the Donner Party museum) sits Truckee Donner Lodge. The discounted rates are $209 plus tax per night for a One King bed room and $239 plus tax per night for a Two Queen bed room. DONNER LAKE INN

This is a small five room bed and breakfast (two rooms are queen beds, two are queen + twin and one family room with a queen and a set of bunk beds for kids under 18). Queen rooms are $169 per night, Queen + twin $179 per night and the family room is $189-$199 per night. The rate includes parking, WiFi, hot tub, afternoon snack, and full hot cooked breakfast.
