Tasman Photos

Photos from the Tasman region of the South Island of New Zealand

We drove over the Takaka Hill mountain pass, which reaches 791 meters above sea level, in the northwest section of the South Island.

We saw the Te Waikoropupū Springs, which are the largest cold water springs in the Southern Hemisphere and contain some of the clearest water ever measured.

After that we stayed at a friend’s beachside farm, where we hung out with the sheep and ducks.
