Christcurch Photos

Photos from Christchurch

Christchurch is NZ's 3rd largest city (largest city in the South Island). Historically, Christchurch was regarded as NZ's most English-like city. After the 2011 earthquake, much of the city was destroyed. As such, today it has a different vibe: there are many vacant lots, semi-destroyed buildings, graffiti and wall murals. Oddly enough, it has reminded me a bit of Berlin from the early 2000s...
